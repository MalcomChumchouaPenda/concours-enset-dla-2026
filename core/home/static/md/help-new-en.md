
1. Go to the home page and **click on "New Registration"** to access the registration page.
2. **Create your registration** by entering the payment number and a password to begin your registration.
3. **Complete the registration form** by describing your application and providing your personal information.
4. **Confirm your registration** and keep your payment number and password safe for any future changes.

**Important:** 

1. You must have paid the examination fees before you can register. 
2. Once validated, the choice of examination centre and option cannot be changed. 
3. A payment number can only be used once.

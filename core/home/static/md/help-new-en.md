
1. Go to the home page and **click on "New Registration"** to access the registration page.
2. **Create your registration** by describing the payment receipt and entering a password to begin your registration.
3. **Fill in registration form** by describing your application and providing your personal information.
4. **Confirm your registration** and keep your payment information and password safe for any future changes.

**Important:** 

1. You must have paid the examination fees before you can register. 
2. Once confirmed, the choice of examination centre and option cannot be changed. 
3. A payment receipt can only be used for one registration.
